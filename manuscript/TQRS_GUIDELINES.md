# TQRS Guidelines (Title — Question — Results — Significance)

This project adopts the TQRS structure to keep manuscripts concise, evidence-focused, and reproducible. Use these rules when drafting in `manuscript/sections/`.

1) (Research) Topic

2) (Research) Question
- Explicit research question(s) or hypotheses. Define the unit of analysis (motions, parties), the timeframe, and measurable outcomes.

3) Rationale
- Why the study was made

4) Significance
- What impact does the study have

File layout and conventions
- Keep source text in `manuscript/sections/01_title.md`, `02_question.md`, `03_results.md`, `04_significance.md`.
- Use `manuscript/Makefile` to assemble text and regenerate figures; do not edit compiled PDFs directly.

Examples and minimal checklist
- Each claim in Results must include a parenthetical pointer: (See Figure X — generated by `scripts/visualize_advanced.py`).
- Include a short methods pointer: `scripts/classify.py` + `classifier/scorer.py`.
